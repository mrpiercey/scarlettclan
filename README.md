# Scarlett's Warrior Cats Quest — The 15 Collars 🐾

A point-and-click adventure in the style of **King's Quest VI**, set in the world of
the warrior cats — made for Scarlett's 15th birthday.

One afternoon, Scarlett falls asleep on **Bus 15** riding home from **Henry Clay High
School**... and wakes up at Fourtrees, in the forest of the warrior clans. Lionheart of
StarClan needs her help: a great storm scattered the **15 collars of the first warriors**
across the clans' camps — and tore the path between the worlds. Returning all fifteen to
him is the **only way home**. Four clan camps hold 3 collars each; earn all twelve and
SkyClan's gorge appears with the final three. Every camp contains everything its own
quests need — the things a warrior cat would actually gather: moss, herbs, prey, feathers,
reeds, honeycomb.

## How to play

**Just double-click `index.html`** — it opens in your browser. No installs, no internet needed.
Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge all work. Click once on the title screen to start
(that first click also turns on the music).

### Controls

| Control | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Left-click** | Walk / do the current action |
| **1 / 2 / 3 / 4** | Switch action: Walk / Look / Talk / Use |
| **5** | Use the item you're carrying |
| **TAB** (or I) | Open your backpack |
| **Right-click** | Also cycles actions: Walk → Look → Talk → Use |
| **Mouse to top of screen** | Icon bar (all actions + backpack, map, music) |
| **Click a screen edge** | Walk off-screen into the neighboring territory |
| **M** | Open the travel map (fast travel) |
| **S** | Music on/off |
| **Space / Enter** | Advance dialogue |

### Tips
- **Talk (3) to every cat** — they tell you exactly what they need.
- Sparkles ✨ mark things you can pick up — grab them with the **hand (4)** or just click them with the **pointer (1)**.
- In the backpack (TAB): click an item, then click it **again** to carry it — then click
  it on a cat or thing in the world (5 re-selects it later).
- Everything a quest needs is found **in the same camp** — no long fetch trips.
- Lost? **Lionheart waits at Fourtrees** and always knows what to do next.
- The game **saves automatically** — close it anytime and choose CONTINUE later.
- A **ding** means you picked something up. A **fanfare** means a collar is yours!

## The world

Six places: **Fourtrees** (where Lionheart waits), the camps of **ThunderClan**,
**RiverClan**, **WindClan**, and **ShadowClan** (3 collars each)... and, once twelve
collars shine, **SkyClan's gorge** with the final three. Hand all fifteen back to
Lionheart, and Scarlett wakes on Bus 15 at the end of the school day — where Dad, Mom,
Hank, and Ramona are waiting outside the dark blue house.

*Happy 15th birthday, Scarlett!* ❤️
