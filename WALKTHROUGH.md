# Walkthrough (SPOILERS — for parental emergencies only!)

**You may never need this:** the glowing **? (HELP)** button on the top bar gives
Scarlett a direct idea about any unsolved puzzle on the current screen, and every
cat's chatter ends with a hint about the camp's remaining tasks.

Every collar: **talk to the cat first** (press 3), then fetch what they need. Items are
picked up with **Use (4)** — and every camp contains everything its own quests need, so
nothing ever has to be carried between camps. Give items by carrying them
(TAB → click item twice) and clicking them on the cat/thing.

## Intro
1. Click through the Bus 15 cutscene — Scarlett dozes off riding home from her summer swim meet.
2. She wakes at Fourtrees: **talk to Lionheart** (press 3, click the golden starry cat).
   He explains the storm scattered the fifteen collars — returning them all to him is the
   only way home. The **map** unlocks (M).

## ThunderClan Camp (1–3)
1. **Cinderpelt**: pick the **marigold** by the nursery → give to Cinderpelt.
2. **Snowkit**: grab the **fallen branch** below Highrock → use it on the **old oak**.
3. **Mousefur**: peel **moss** from the bank by the warriors' den → use it on the **small hole** by the fresh-kill pile.

## RiverClan Camp (4–6)
4. Pick up the **driftwood branch** on the bank → use it on the **river** to hook out the collar.
5. Reach into the gap under the **smooth stones** (hand) → give the **shiny stone** to **Mosspelt**.
6. Cut **reeds** + collect the **swan feathers** on the bank → use both on **Graypool's nest**.

## WindClan Camp (7–9)
7. Pick the **sweet clover** in the camp hollow → use it on the **rabbit warren**.
8. Talk to **Morningflower** (the tick!) → take the **mouse-bile leaf** from **Barkface's herb store** → use it on Morningflower.
9. Roll a **moss-ball** from the mossy boulder → give it to **Gorsepaw** (his first catch earns the collar).

## ShadowClan Camp (10–12)
10. Catch the **frog** at the camp pond (hand) → give to **Russetfur**.
11. Talk to **Littlecloud** (snake-charm herb) → use the herb on **Snakerocks**, then grab the **glow-moss** → give to **Blackstar**.
12. Use the **glow-moss** on the **old fox den** to find Smokekit.

## SkyClan Camp (13–15) — appears on the map at 12 collars
13. Talk to **Leafstar** → dig the **patch of turned earth** below the cliff caves → return the ancestors' collar to Leafstar.
14. Scoop the **spring-soaked moss** → use it on the **wild beehive** in the gorge wall → give the **honeycomb** to **Sharpclaw**.
15. Talk to **Echosong** and answer the Trial of Trust: **RiverClan / a kittypet / the Gathering truce**.

## Going home
With all fifteen collars, **return to Fourtrees and TALK to Lionheart**. Hand him the
collars, enjoy the ceremony... and wake up on Bus 15 at the end of the school day, where
Dad, Mom, Hank, and Ramona are waiting outside the dark blue house. 🎂
